About Jasper AI

Formerly Jarvis, Jasper offers 50+ templates for product descriptions, blog posts, video scripts, email sequences, and ad copy. Its “Boss Mode” UI allows users to guide content generation seamlessly. Jasper emphasizes brand tone consistency and integrates with Surfer SEO for optimization. Ideal for agencies and large teams, Jasper supports team collaboration and real-time editing, producing polished long-form content efficiently.
